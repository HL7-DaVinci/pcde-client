PCDE Client

This server provides a client for interacting with the Da Vinci Payer Coverage Decision Exchange Reference Implementation.

To use a different part of the client click on one of links on the side menu.

FHIR Endpoint

All requests are made to the endpoint: https://davinci-pcde-ri.logicahealth.org/fhir/Patient

To retrieve sample patients, search with names Joe Smith or Jeff Smith. Jeff Smith has two results so the birthdays, 1980-01-12 or 1980-01-13, can be used to refine the search.
